Rise Against Hunger Learning Survey (HackNC 2020)

I worked with a team of three other wonderful women from my school to build a web app prototype for Rise Against Hunger, a local non-profit battling food insecurity. The end product would be a WordPress-compatible interactive quiz on various facts about the scope of world hunger, with a contact form at the end. For my part, I coded a JavaScript version of the quiz which provided the user with various resources depending on their choice of learning path as well as experimented with PHP to help create a Wordpress plugin. Our prototype won both the HackNC 2020 Nonprofit Challenge and placed in the top 25 overall to win the Wolfram Award.

Byte's School Survival Hacks (TechTogether New York 2020)

This was another team project with three amazing women from NC State! We built an app prototype to help freshmen navigate their first year of college, which included a GPA calculator, a quiz to find your learning style, forums to meet other students, and an admin portal for faculty. I built a quiz in JavaScript to identify user learning styles based on user answers and provide resources to find how you learn best based on quiz results, and styled webpages using HTML/CSS.
