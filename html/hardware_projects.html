Simple LED Circuit

This was one of the first circuits I ever built. Using voltage from the Analog Discovery 2 power source, I series-connected a potentiometer and resistor and connected these to an LED, with the result being the ability to dim/brighten the LED by turning the dial of the potentiometer.

Simple Speaker Circuit

This circuit, given a sound file in .wav format, outputs the audio in an audible form. This is accomplished by series-connecting a capacitor to the speaker, which "cleans up" the sound by filtering out the DC component of the sound, leaving only the AC component to be blasted by the speaker.
